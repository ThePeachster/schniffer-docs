What is Schniffer?

cold, hard facts

In 2015, over two-thirds of businesses targeted in a cyber attack were small and family-run businesses. The worst data breaches can cause between $84,000 and $148,000 of damage, leading to 60% of businesses folding within a year of the breach.

the problem

Small business owners are often willfully ignorant to the threats of breaches, deeming it too costly and unnecessary, leaving them prime-targets to cyber-attacks.

our solution

Schniffer is a low-cost, all-encompassing network monitoring and vulnerability scanning tool, acting as a first line of defense for small-businesses against cyber-threats. Simply install Schniffer on the network to begin protecting your small business.

Schniffer features

Vulnerability scanning

Learn about your network vulnerabilities before attackers do

Packet sniffing

Watches your network traffic for suspicious events that could be indicative of an attack. Receive real-time alerts when suspicious events occur.

Wifi speed test

Test network speed to detect anomalies and down times

Users must login to view network data
